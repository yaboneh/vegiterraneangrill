Our father, Andreas Demetriou, came to the United States as a young boy in the 60’s with his mother, our Yia-Yia, to restart their lives. As a way to give back to the community, he started this grill to share authentic Greek cuisine with the greater LA area, filling the air with the scent of smokey shawarma, unique textures of the crispy, yet simultaneously soft falafel, and other delectable items to have you feeling the rustling of olive trees and the mist from the Mediterranean all in the way in Southern California. “Meet us at our location in Burbank next to McCambridge Park.”
