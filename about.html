As times changed, our family-owned business shifted to a more sustainable and animal-friendly menu. Animal husbandry is becoming increasingly cruel while directly affecting climate change, which is gaining momentum daily. Becoming vegan gave us hope that our decision, though small, could help bring change in the way we consume our food. Our kitchen caters to everyone, vegan or not, so non-veg-lovers can still enjoy their favorite Mediterranean dishes without being able to distinguish between real and substitute meat. We collaborate with local farms Urban Homestead in Pasadena, Forneris Farms in Mission Hills, and ECo Urban Gardens in Los Angeles to deliver fresh produce directly from the source straight to your plate.
